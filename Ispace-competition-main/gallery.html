Hall Of Fame

At iSPACE, we believe in recognizing and celebrating the exceptional talents displayed by young minds in the field of coding. Our virtual competition is not just a platform for learning; it's a stage where creativity, innovations and techni prowess are celebrated. We take immense pride in honoring participants who have demonstrated outstanding skills through our awards program.

Amazing Prizes!
If winning the animators being crowned best Young Animator of the Year UK wasn't enough, winners will be invited to special tours of the world-famous Aardman Animations in Bristol, Blue Zoo Animation Studio in London and AXIS Studios in Glasgow to discover how professional animations are made.
The Awards Ceremony
The winning films will be announced and premiered at Manchester Animation Festival (the UK's biggest aniamtion festival) in November, at a special awards ceremony. Animators that have been shortlisted to win will be notified in early October so they can arrange to attend.
Trophies
The six winners of this year's Young Animator of the Year Competition will receive one of these hand-crafted trophies, created by One6th Animation Studio in Manchester.
Thanks to ScreenSkills Animation Skills Fund for sponsoring this year's awards.
