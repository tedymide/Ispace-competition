About Us

The acronym iSPACE means Introduction to Systematic Programming, Animation, Computer Engineering and Electronics .

iSPACE COMPETITION

iSPACE COMPETITION is an academic program designed to train and encourage students in a career pathway and also develop innovative their creative abilities / skills that will be useful in areas problem solving. We aim to achieve this by creating a competitive environment for children in these following areas:

  • Programming: This consist of Mobile Apps development, Games development, Web Development.
  • Animation: By bringing out the creative skills in young ones through Graphics Character Design and Character Animation.
  • Robotics (Drone-Programming, Codey-Rockey, Mbot, Robotics Smart Marchine, Lego EV-3 and so many others.),Electronics Circuit Design

At iSPACE Competition, we offer different varieties of competitions, such as;

  • CODEFEST
  • DIGITAL LITERACY
  • ROBOTICS
  • VIRTUAL COMPETITIONS

At iSPACE Competition, we believe in creating a space where students from different schools, within schools, and even those participating virtually can come together to exhibit their skills and innovative spirit. Whether it's academic prowess, artistic flair, or technological innovation, we provide a stage for every student to shine.
